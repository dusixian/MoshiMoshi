## üì≤ How to Install

Since this is a beta release, the app is distributed via Apple's TestFlight platform.

### Prerequisites
* An iPhone running **iOS 16.0** or later.
* The email address associated with your Apple ID (must be invited by our team).
* **Internet Connection** (Required for AI agent communication).

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Check Your Email
You should have received an invitation email from **TestFlight** (Apple).
* **Subject:** "Inception Studio LLC has invited you to test MoshiMoshi"
* *Note: If you don't see it, please check your Spam/Junk folder.*

#### 2. Download TestFlight
If you haven't already, download the **TestFlight** app from the App Store.
* [Download TestFlight on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/testflight/id899247664)

#### 3. Install MoshiMoshi
1.  Open the invitation email on your iPhone.
2.  Tap the blue **"View in TestFlight"** button.
3.  This will launch the TestFlight app. Tap **INSTALL** next to the MoshiMoshi icon.
4.  Once installed, the app will appear on your home screen with a yellow dot, indicating a beta version.
